Maternity Alert-Monitor

Maternity Alert-Monitor is a monitoring software that enables early medical intervention by midwives through AI-based automatic interpretation of CTG alerts, even in cases of healthcare worker shortages and inadequate training.
It allows 24/7 patient monitoring, enabling healthcare providers with limited CTG training to perform immediate medical interventions by giving color and sound alerts.

The key features of Maternity Alert-Monitor are as follows:

  • Real time monitoring of fetal heart rate and uterine contraction and achieves high computing accuracy in interpretation using AI
  • Detects even the smallest changes in variability and deceleration and gives alerts
  • Encourages earlier medical intervention by midwives

Background

spiker ltd,. is a startup specializing in AI medical equipment manufacturing established in Japan in 2020. One of our founders has 9+ years of business experience in Africa and another is an ObsGyn doctor. Since 2020, spiker has been conducting extensive research and market surveys in Kenya and other African countries, collaborating with obstetricians to incorporate their feedback into the product and partnering with AA Health Dynamics.

Through our study, we found many hospitals still face the challenges of understaffing and inadequate training.

Monitoring with CTG is particularly important for high-risk obstetric patients, and there is no doubt that early medical intervention by midwives is crucial for maintaining and restoring the well-being of the fetus. In Japan, CTG has been widely used since the 1960s, and by the 1980s, it was utilized in almost all deliveries, contributing to Japan's perinatal mortality rate being the lowest in the world at 3 per 1000 births.

However, with Kenya having only about one-tenth of the healthcare workers compared to Japan, achieving the same level of care is challenging.
